The program includes a 3D labyrinth, a ground plane, Phong shading, and a directional light source. Pac-Man is modeled as an animated sphere that continuously opens and closes its mouth, and his eyes move according to the mouth animation. Pac-Man also rotates smoothly to face the direction of movement.

The graphical requirements are covered through a shear-view camera mode that can be toggled, as well as a shadow projection drawn onto the ground plane. These help visualize the 3D structure of the scene clearly.

Gameplay elements include continuous movement, wall collisions, collectible dots, a win condition, a jumping mechanic, a camera that follows Pac-Man, and 4 ghosts that move around the maze. The ghosts always orient their eyes toward Pac-Man and interact with him on collision, causing a short freeze and blinking of the Pac-Man before the game resets.

Additional features include scoring, lives, sound effects on actions like death, kill, eating and winning, power mode, and temporary edible ghost behavior. Ghosts change to only-eye state when eaten, return to spawn position and are not dangerous for 3 seconds which is represented by blinking animation.

Controls consist of arrow keys for movement, space for jumping, V for toggling shear view, and S for sound toggling.
